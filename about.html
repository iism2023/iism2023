IISM2023 | About
Embark on a thrilling journey at the 10th INTER IISER Sports Meet (IISM), an extraordinary convergence of athletic prowess and companionship hosted by IISER Thiruvananthapuram! This landmark event brings together students from all IISERs, NISER, and CEBs, transcending boundaries and marking a decade of sporting excellence. IISM is not merely a competition; it's a dynamic canvas where talent meets teamwork, and friendships are woven on the track and field. Proudly hosted by IISER Thiruvananthapuram, this edition that will be held from 23rd-29th December,2023 promises an unforgettable experience against the vibrant backdrop of Kerala's rich culture. Beyond academics, IISM is a celebration of physical well-being, community spirit, and the joy of healthy competition. Immerse yourself in this extraordinary celebration where excellence unfolds, hosted by IISER Thiruvananthapuram, the pulsating heart of this year's Inter IISER Sports Meet!
